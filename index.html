POPULAR QUESTIONS

  • Will my vehicle be the same after the repair?

    Yes! We have the equipment and training to restore your vehicle to pre-loss condition so be cosmetically and structurally equal to how it was before the accident.

  • Who pays the repair bills?

    You must arrange for the payment. Your insurance policy will state that the insurance company will pay you less any deductibles or depreciation.

  • Shouldn't I go back to the dealers for repairs?

    Your dealer may not be your best choice for collision repair. Quality control and personal attention can sometimes be difficult for the big dealer shop, as 30 or 40 repairs are juggled simultaneously.

    Rather than automatically returning to your dealer for collision repairs, we encourage you to be a careful shopper when selecting a repair facility. Ask your friends, neighbors, and relatives about the experiences they have had with collision repair, and select the shop you think will provide the highest quality result.
